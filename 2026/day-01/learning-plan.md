# What is your understanding of DevOps and Cloud Engineering?

DevOps is a cultural mindset and a set of practices that unifies software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops). Its primary goal is to break down team silos and automate the entire Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)—from planning and writing code to deployment and monitoring. It focuses on maximizing velocity, improving collaboration, and ensuring application reliability.

Cloud Engineering is the technical discipline focused on designing, building, managing and optimizing infrastructure on public or private cloud platforms. Cloud engineers focus heavily on the underlying architecture—ensuring that databases, virtual networks, and storage systems are highly available, secure, scalable, and cost-effective.
